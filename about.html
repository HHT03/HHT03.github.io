Hi, my name is Hung Tran

I am passionate about designing.

Born in Vietnam and came to the United States when I was just six years old. My fascination with design has been a constant and genuine passion in my life. Yes, design is very broad but I do value and am passionate about all types of designs, such as graphic design, UI/UX design, product design, and structural design. I firmly believe that the concept of "design" has the potential to profoundly impact and revolutionize our world through its universal language.

In addition to my love for design, I am also passionate about other hobbies. Photography is one of them, it acts as a reason for me to go outside and enjoy the outdoors, capture moments with friends and family, and the different aspects of life. Throughout my life, music creation has remained a hobby. I have faced many hardships throughout my childhood and music, to me, serves as a form of therapy, and my passion for both listening to other artists and creating it myself. Last but not least, my clothing brand. Initially started out as a centralized music media brand and turned into a clothing brand, I wanted to spread positivity around the world. My clothing brand BAND!T allows me to spread that message while allowing me to practice my designing skills. With my diverse interests and unwavering commitment to creative pursuits, I am excited to bring my unique perspective and talents to the world!
